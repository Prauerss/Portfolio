Elysian Siege: The Prince's Uprising

Epic Action-Adventure Game Development

Unreal Engine 5 C++ Blueprints Level Design Team Project

Project Overview

A comprehensive look at the development process and challenges faced during the creation of Elysian Siege

Game Concept & Vision

This project was developed collaboratively by a dedicated team. The game revolves around a central protagonist who must reach her ultimate objective by overcoming a series of challenges. To do so, she relies on a variety of carefully designed mechanics that shape the player's experience and progression. Each mechanic was created to reinforce the gameplay loop and to make the journey engaging, rewarding, and thematically consistent.

My Contributions to the Project

Enemy AI & Combat

Implemented ranged enemy behavior and mechanics, including player detection, shooting decisions, and environmental interactions. Balanced aggressiveness to create engaging combat encounters.

Audio Integration

Handled complete audio implementation across menus and gameplay, including background music, sound effects, and feedback sounds to enhance player immersion and interaction.

Visual Effects

Created and integrated particle systems for explosion effects, NPC defeats, and pickup interactions. Designed to make interactions more dynamic and visually satisfying.

Collectibles System

Implemented all game pickups with unique benefits and programmed smooth curve animations for collection feedback, adding polish and making item collection more rewarding.

Pool System

An object pool system was implemented to optimize performance by pre-instantiating and reusing pickups, enemies, and particles. The system is distance-based, allowing for efficient management of large levels without degrading framerate.

Development Challenges & Solutions

AI Behavior Balance

Gameplay Programming
Challenge

Creating ranged enemies that felt challenging but fair. Initial AI was either too aggressive (frustrating) or too passive (boring), affecting the game's tension and pacing.

Solution

Implemented a dynamic behavior system with multiple states: patrol, detection and combat. Added randomization to shooting patterns to create unpredictable but manageable encounters.

Audio Synchronization

Technical Integration
Challenge

Ensuring audio cues matched gameplay events precisely. Delayed or misaligned sound effects broke immersion and made combat feel disconnected from player actions.

Solution

Created a centralized audio manager system. Implemented audio pooling and precise timing mechanisms to ensure perfect synchronization between actions and sounds.

Performance Optimization

Technical Optimization
Challenge

Particle effects were causing frame rate drops, especially during intense combat scenarios with multiple explosions and pickups on screen simultaneously.

Solution

Implemented object pooling for particle systems, optimized particle count based on distance from camera.

Pickup Animation Flow

User Experience
Challenge

Creating smooth, satisfying pickup animations that felt rewarding without interrupting gameplay flow or causing collision issues with the collection system.

Solution

Developed a curve-based animation system using CurveVector for natural movement, with collision detection disabled during animation and visual feedback that scales with pickup value.
