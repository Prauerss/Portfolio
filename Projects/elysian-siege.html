Elysian Siege: The Prince's Uprising

Epic Action-Adventure Game Development

Unreal Engine 5 C++ Blueprints Level Design Team Project

Project Overview

This project was developed collaboratively by a dedicated team. The game revolves around a central protagonist who must reach her ultimate objective by overcoming a series of challenges. To do so, she relies on a variety of carefully designed mechanics that shape the player’s experience and progression. Each mechanic was created to reinforce the gameplay loop and to make the journey engaging, rewarding, and thematically consistent.

My Role in the Project

Within this project, my primary contributions focused on gameplay programming, audio integration, and particle effects. My tasks included:

Enemy Ranged Behavior & Mechanics

I was responsible for implementing the behavior and mechanics of the ranged enemy type. This involved designing how the enemy detects the player, when and how it decides to shoot, and how it interacts with the environment during combat encounters. Balancing the enemy’s aggressiveness while ensuring fair gameplay was key to creating tension and excitement for the player.

Audio Implementation

I handled the integration of all in-game audio, both in the menus and in the gameplay itself. This included background music, sound effects for actions and events, and feedback sounds that enhance player interaction. The goal was to ensure that audio elements not only complemented the visuals but also heightened immersion and player engagement.

Particle Effects Creation

I contributed to the visual effects of the game by creating and integrating several particle systems. Among these were the explosion effects for defeated NPCs, as well as visual feedback for pickups scattered across the game world. These effects were designed to make interactions more dynamic and visually satisfying for the player.

Pickups & Collection Curves

I implemented all of the pickups available in the game, each of which provides the player with unique benefits. To enhance the collection experience, I also programmed the smooth curve animation that triggers when a pickup is collected. This small detail adds polish and makes item collection feel more rewarding and impactful.
